Gudi Padwa

Gudi Padwa is a traditional Marathi New Year festival celebrated in Maharashtra, India.

Significance

It marks the beginning of the new year and is celebrated with great enthusiasm and fervor.

Traditions

  • Raising the Gudi (a decorated flag or banner)
  • Wearing new clothes
  • Exchanging sweets and gifts
  • Traditional food and decorations

Wishes

Happy Gudi Padwa! May this new year bring prosperity, happiness, and good fortune.
